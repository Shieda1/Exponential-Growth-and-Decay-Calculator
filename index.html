What is the exponential growth and decay?
the exponential growth and decay is an exponential growth when the base of the exponential is bigger than 1 and this means that those numbers get bigger. It's exponential decay when the base of the exponential is in between 1 and 0 and these numbers get smaller.
How to calculate exponential growth and decay?
To calculate exponential growth and decay you can use the following formulas respectively:
f(x) = a(1 + r)^t
f(x) = a(1 - r)^t
where:
r = rate of growth
t = time steps
Why use exponential growth and decay?
The exponential growth is used for applications in studying bacterial growth, population increase, money growth schemes, while the exponential decay is used to find food decay, half-life, radioactive decay.
